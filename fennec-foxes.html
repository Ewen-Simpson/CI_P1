Fennec Foxes

Vulpes zerda

Where do fennec foxes live?

Fennec foxes are the smallest species of true foxes. Their native habitat is in the Sahara Desert and the Sinai Peninsula.

3 facts about fennec foxes

  1. Fennec foxes have the largest ears proportinate to body size of not only any fox species, but of any canid species. They use their ears to help keep themselves cool in their desert environment.

  2. Fennec foxes have thick fur on the bottom of their paws so that they do not get burned by hot sand.

  3. Although native to the Sahara Desert, fennec foxes are the only mammal native to the area that is known to be able to survive without a standing water source. This is because their kidneys are adapted to get extra moisture from their food and that their burrowing behaviour helps to produce dew that they can consume.
